Create a New Category

When you’re building out your Item Library, categories make it easier for staff and customers to find what they’re looking for. This guide walks you through creating a new category and setting optional details like an icon or subcategories.

1. Open the Item Library

From the main Item Library page, look to the left panel where your categories are displayed. Click the + button next to the “Categories” heading. This opens the “Create Category” screen.

2. Enter a Category Name

In the “Category Info” section, type a clear, descriptive name for your new category. For example, “Hot Drinks,” “Office Supplies,” or “Lunch Combos.”

3. Choose an Image, Icon, or No Image

You can decide how you want the category to appear:

  • Image: Upload your own graphic or photo (for instance, a cup of coffee for “Hot Drinks”).
  • Icon: Pick from our preset icons to keep a uniform look across categories.
  • No Image: Stick with a simple, text‐only display.

4. Select a Colour Code

Adding a colour helps you quickly spot categories at a glance. Use bright colours for breakfast items, or a calm green for healthy options. This colour appears alongside the category name to make it stand out.

5. Add Subcategories

If you want to break things down further, click Add Subcategory and name it. A “Hot Drinks” category could have “Tea” and “Coffee” as subcategories. This extra layer of organisation helps when you have a lot of items.

6. Save Your Category

Once you’ve filled in the details, click Save. Your new category appears in the left panel of your Item Library, ready for you to add items or make changes later.

Why Use Colours or Subcategories?

Colours let staff quickly distinguish between sections (like a bright colour for breakfast). Subcategories make larger menus more manageable, so a big category like “Hot Foods” can be split into “Soups,” “Wraps,” or “Sandwiches.” Even in a small operation, planning now can save time later—keeping everything organised as your business grows.
